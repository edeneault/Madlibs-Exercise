Madlibs - What are they?

Mad Libs is a phrasal template word game which consists of one player prompting others for a list of words to substitute for blanks in a story before reading aloud.

How to play?

Choose a Story from the dropdown menu above. Enter all the requested words with the appropriate type, ex: noun, verb, color, animal, etc.. Once completed, submit your words and see your madlib story.

Example:

"___________! he said ________ as he jumped into his convertible ______ and drove off with his _________ wife."

In this example, the required words would be of the following types: "exclamation, adverb, noun, adjective"
